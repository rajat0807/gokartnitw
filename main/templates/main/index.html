About Us

National Institute of Technology Warangal, one of India’s premier education institute was established in 1959 under the name of Regional Engineering College. In 2002, it was declared as an
institute of national importance
. Outlook Survey 2015 ranks it
11th
among the technical institutes of the country. The vision of the college is to give competent engineers, technocrats and researchers to various sectors of industry.

Vision

What started as a passion is now a dream, a dream to design and
build a state of the art go-kart that is stronger, sturdier and
lighter than ever before. Using top of the line materials we
aim to exhibit higher endurance, stability, resilience and
reach speeds never touched before. Our wish is to
extract the maximum we can get out of this
experience be it knowledge or the bonding we have
within our
MechXhausters
family.

Go Kart Design Challenge

GKDC is a contest formulated to enhance the approach of students practicing Engineering courses. This event is fundamentally all about designing and fabricating a Go Kart at very low expenditure. The teams participating in this contest have to go through two rounds: Pre-Final Round and Final Round.

Students Kart Design Challenge

SKDC is a competition initiated to enhance better engineering approaches in undergraduate students and exhibit their creativity and engineering skills. The vehicle and associated documentation are researched, designed and fabricated by the team themselves. The competition encourages participants to showcase their innovation proving along with it the practical and economical viability of their project.
